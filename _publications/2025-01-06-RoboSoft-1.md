We propose a geometry-aware attention network (GAANet) for end-to-end static modeling of tendon-driven continuum robots with hyper-redundant DOFs, integrating domain-specific geometric constraints and demonstrably outperforming a multilayer perceptron (MLP) and a pseudo rigid body model (PRBM) in terms of tip position accuracy and real-time feasibility.
